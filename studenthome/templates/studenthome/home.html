Vision and Mission

Vision: The core graduate school of exemplary professionals.

Mission: To cultivate value-laden professionals by advancing instruction, research, publication, and extension.

Core Values, Goals, and Objectives

Core Values
  • Excellence - Serving towards superior quality and global competitiveness.
  • Commitment - Doing work with love and inspiration.
  • Social Responsibility - Actively accepting responsibility with integrity.

Goals and outcomes are aligned with quality graduate education and community-centered impact.

Objectives:
  1. Provide advanced and multidisciplinary learning opportunities.
  2. Develop critical, creative, and collaborative lifelong learners.
  3. Produce research that addresses real community needs.

Goals

  1. To provide opportunities for advancement in professional growth and development through effective, efficient, flexible, and enriched instructional approaches in the graduate and post-graduate degree programs.
  2. To produce capable, proficient, and intellectual graduates who are respected managers and trusted leaders in their respective fields of work.
  3. To generate quality and relevant researches that will be utilized to respond to societal issues and challenges effectively.

Objectives

The University of Bohol Graduate School aims to produce graduates who are:

  • well-equipped with highly advanced knowledge and skills in the specialized field of disciplines and programs through multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary approaches;
  • critical, creative, and collaborative in pursuing lifelong learning; and
  • highly competent in producing researches utilized effectively to address community problems and issues.

Outcomes

By the time of graduation, the graduates shall have the ability to:

  • utilize professional expertise to create innovations in management, systems improvement, policy, and program formulation;
  • engage in lifelong learning for career advancement and leadership;
  • adhere to the high ethical and moral standards in the practice of one's profession; and
  • publish original research for utilization in community development.
